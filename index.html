About The App

Eduvos' Official Navigation Technique

VosGo is a smart wayfinding app designed to make navigating campuses, buildings, and complex spaces effortless. With real-time guidance, integrated timetables, and instant access to student advisors, VosGo helps students stay organized, connected, and stress-free. Whether you're new to campus or just need quick directions, VosGo ensures you always know where to go and what's happening next.
